Import BibTeX bibliography easily

How to use:

  • Open a Reference Work form in Specify 7
  • Copy a BibTeX entry to the clipboard
  • Click "Import BibTeX"
  • Fields will be filled automatically!

Compatible fields:

Title, Publisher, Journal, Year, Volume, Pages, Authors, and more.

BibTeX example:

@article{darwin1859, title={On the Origin of Species}, author={Darwin, Charles}, journal={John Murray}, year={1859}, publisher={London} }
